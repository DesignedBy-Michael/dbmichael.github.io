Design Process

My structured approach ensures clarity, collaboration, and creativity at every step. By combining my design expertise with your unique vision, we’ll create something that not only looks amazing but also resonates with your audience.

Discovery

This is where we dive deep into your brand, your goals, and your audience. I’ll ask questions, listen to your ideas, and get to know the heart of your business. Think of it as laying the foundation for everything we’ll build together.

  • Initial consultation
  • Goal setting and project scope
  • Brand questionnaire

Creative Strategy

With a clear understanding of your needs, I’ll develop a tailored strategy to guide the project. This includes research, mood boards, and a roadmap to ensure we’re aligned on the creative direction.

  • Mood Board
  • Market Research
  • Stragety Presentation

Creation

This is where the magic happens! Using the insights from the discovery and strategy phases, I’ll design concepts that reflect your brand’s unique identity. You’ll see your vision start to take shape.

  • Concept development
  • Collaborative Feedback
  • Iterative Design Work

Showcase

Once the designs are finalized, I’ll present them in a way that highlights their impact. Whether it’s a brand identity, logo, or motion design, you’ll see how everything comes together to tell your story.

  • Motion design previews (if applicable)
  • Project mock ups (e.g., business cards, social media, websites)
  • Final design presentation

Off-Boarding

The project doesn’t end with delivery—I’ll make sure you have everything you need to succeed. From file hand-offs to brand guidelines, I’ll set you up for long-term success.

  • Final review and support
  • Brand guidelines (if applicable)
  • File delivery (print-ready and digital formats)

Post Project support

Even after the project wraps up, I’m here for you. Whether you need extra guidance, help with implementation, or ongoing design support, I’m committed to building lasting relationships with my clients. Don’t hesitate to reach out—I’m just a message away!

Lets get to work!
